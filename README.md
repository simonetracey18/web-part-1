 Project Overview
This project involves the design and development of a website for *Pretty and Clawed*, a nail business based in Johannesburg South. The purpose of the website is to create a professional online presence and provide clients with easy access to information about services, pricing, and contact details.

The website is developed using HTML and consists of multiple interconnected pages to ensure a smooth and user-friendly experience.

 Website Goals and Objectives
- Create a professional online presence for the business 
- Provide clear information about services and pricing 
- Allow users to enquire and book appointments 
- Showcase nail designs through images 
- Improve customer engagement and accessibility 

 Key Features and Functionality
- Homepage with introduction and call-to-action 
- About Us page with business background, mission, and vision 
- Services page displaying nail services with images and pricing 
- Enquiry page with a form for service-related enquiries 
- Contact page with multiple locations, contact details, and form 
- Navigation bar linking all pages 
- Image integration to showcase nail designs
