About Pretty and Clawed

our history

PRETTY AND CLAWED is a Johannesburg-based founded with the passion for beauty,creativity and self expression. The business started as a small home-based service and quickly grew through social media,especially instagram,where unique designs and satisfied clients helped build a strong reputation.today PRETTY AND CLAWED continuebto grow as a trusted nail brand in Johannesburg South.
